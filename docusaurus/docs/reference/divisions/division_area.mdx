# Division Area

A division area is a polygon that represents the land or maritime area associated with a [division](division).


<table>
  <tbody>
  <tr>
    <th>Geometry Type</th>
    <td><code>Polygon</code> or <code>MultiPolygon</code></td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <th>Theme</th>
    <td><code>divisions</code></td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <th>Type</th>
    <td><code>division_area</code></td>
  </tr>
  </tbody>
</table>

## Sub-types

Like a division, a division area may have one of two possible sub-types: `country` or `region`.

<Tabs>
  <TabItem value="country" label="Country" default>
    <table>
      <tbody>
        <tr>
          <th><code>subtype</code></th>
          <td><code>country</code></td>
        </tr>
      </tbody>
    </table>


The border of a country, if known, is given by
[boundary](boundary) features.
  </TabItem>

  <TabItem value="region" label="Region">
    <table>
      <tbody>
        <tr>
          <th><code>subtype</code></th>
          <td><code>region</code></td>
        </tr>
      </tbody>
    </table>

A region represents a named area that is not formally part of
the hierarchical subdivision of a country.
  </TabItem>
</Tabs>
